DISSEMINATION ACTIVITIES

I think there is a difference between what people thinks researchers do and what we really do. A possible way to decrease the current distance between research and society consists of the dissemination activities, where research is explained in a way such that it can be understood by the most of the people. Here, I present some dissemination activities I have carried out so far.
