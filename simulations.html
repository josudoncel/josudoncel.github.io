Bandwidth Sharing in TCP

The TCP protocol controls the flow of traffic in the Internet. TCP reacts to congestion and adapts dynamically the transmission rate to the conditions of the network. A key parameter in a transmission is the round-trip-time, that is, the time it takes a packet to travel back and forth from origin to destination.
